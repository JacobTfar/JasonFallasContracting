About us

Trusted New Jersey Contractor

Our Story

Founded in New Jersey, Jason Fallas Contracting brings over 15 years of experience to every project we undertake. What started as a small family business has grown into a trusted name in residential and commercial contracting throughout the state. We believe that quality workmanship, attention to detail, and clear communication are the foundations of successful projects.

Our team consists of skilled professionals who take pride in their craft. From kitchen renovations to full home remodels, we approach each project with the same level of dedication and expertise. We understand that your property is important to you, which is why we treat every space as if it were our own.

Our Values

At Jason Fallas Contracting, we operate with a simple set of principles: honesty in our estimates, quality in our work, and respect for our clients' time and property. We adhere to all local building codes and regulations to ensure your project is completed safely and properly.

Jason's journey began in Costa Rica, where he learned the fundamentals of construction working alongside his father. After moving to the United States at a young age, he continued developing his skills working out of Union, NJ with established companies like Monk's Painting and Rutgers Painting. This valuable experience led him to found Jason Fallas Home Improvements in 2010, which has now evolved into Jason Fallas Contracting as we expand our services to support not only residential clients but also local businesses in our community.

We believe in building relationships, not just structures. Many of our clients return to us for multiple projects because they know they can count on our reliability and craftsmanship. From the initial consultation to the final walkthrough, we're committed to making your renovation or building experience as smooth and stress-free as possible.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional contracting services that exceed client expectations through quality craftsmanship, transparent communication, and attention to detail on every project, regardless of size.
